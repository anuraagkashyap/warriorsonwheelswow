About us

We the Warriors on Wheels - WOW are neither optimists nor pessimists, we are possiblists.

Warriors on Wheels - WOW is a Non - Profit Organisation set up specially for people who have locomotive disability and are on wheelchair. This organisation was specially set up for helping the poeple on wheelchair to do all sort of co-curricular activities in particular as normal people do in daily life.
   People who move on wheels have dreams to do many activities which normal people do but they are not able to follow their dreams as no proper platform and arrangements are provided to such people. Warriors on Wheels take care of this problem faced by wheelchair users.

  • Strategy

    The main strategy behind forming Warriors on Wheels - WoW was to bring together all the wheelchair users and help them live a great and productive life.

  • Links

    Warriors on Wheels - WoW is mainly built on its strong links. Strong links with each other is the main strength of Warriors on Wheels - WoW. Spreading our network all around the globe and involving as many people as possible is our main aim.

  • Motivation & Encouragement

    We are the real warriors who have to fight with a number of situations that are being encountered in daily life. From mere entering home through stairs to boarding a bus, challenges are being faced everywhere. We the Warriors on Wheels encourage and motivate the people on wheelchair.
